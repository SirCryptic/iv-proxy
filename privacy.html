Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2025

Overview

ChatApp is a client-side encrypted chat application. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data or messages on our servers. All encryption and decryption occur in your browser.

Data Handling

  • Messages: Encrypted locally using AES-GCM and stored in your browser via localForage. Messages expire after 30 days.
  • Rooms: Room metadata (name, ID) is stored locally and expires after 30 days.
  • Nicknames: Stored locally and not transmitted.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, analytics, or third-party tracking.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption (PBKDF2, AES-GCM). You are responsible for keeping your password secure. Do not share room passwords with unauthorized users.
